Mission & Engineering Vision

Realmate was pioneered by Reychan Bernaldez alongside an elite multi-disciplinary team: Jaztin Pumaren and Danniel Libor (Computer Engineers), Mark Hequibal (Marketing Manager), and Sean Sayson (Accountant & Financial Expert).

By integrating Advanced Artificial Intelligence, we have engineered a solution specifically for Alveo property specialists. We recognize that the modern real estate landscape demands more than manual effort; it requires algorithmic precision to handle complex reselling and portfolio management.

Our platform utilizes AI to analyze market data in real-time, helping specialists find the absolute closest match for their clients' property resale needs. We bridge the gap between financial strategy, technical engineering, and marketing expertise to ensure every transaction is optimized for success.
